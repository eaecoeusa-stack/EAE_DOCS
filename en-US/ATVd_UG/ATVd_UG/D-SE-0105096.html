Firmware Version Compatibility

The ATV dPAC is compatible with:

  • ATV340 drives with, at least, V1.8IE94 software version.

  • Altivar Process ATV600 (ATV630, ATV650) drives with, at least, V2.6IE94 software version

  • Altivar Process ATV900 (ATV930, ATV950) drives with, at least, V2.3IE94 software version.

In case of incompatible version between the drive and the ATV dPAC, [Internal Error 6] (InF6) is triggered. A firmware update can be performed using EcoStruxure Automation Device Maintenance. For more information contact your local Schneider Electric Services.

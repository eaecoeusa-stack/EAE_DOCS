VW3A1111

Graphic Display Terminal (VW3A1111)

  • Real-time clock with a 10 years backup battery, to monitor the time when the power is off.

  • Pixel resolution 240 x 160 with a capacity to display 8 lines of messages.

  • Protection IP65.

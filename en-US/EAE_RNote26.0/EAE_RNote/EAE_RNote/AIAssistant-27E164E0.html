Known Limitations

Format Response

Currently Conversational Help can only provide text-based response i.e. it cannot support images, tables as a prompt/response.

Response Accuracy

This feature is powered by Artificial Intelligence and generates automated responses that might not be always accurate, complete or up to date. Do not rely solely on this information for decision making, including for real-time control, safety-critical decision-making, or emergency response in industrial environments and carefully read the safety instructions as indicated in the documentation.

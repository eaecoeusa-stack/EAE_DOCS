EcoStruxure Automation Processor 310

EcoStruxure Automation Expert offer version 25.0, is pleased to introduce a brand new controller: EcoStruxure™ Automation Processor 310 (AP310). A controller built for today's challenges as wells as for tomorrow's possibilities.

Secure, rugged, intelligent automation at the edge

The reliability and efficiency of industrial control processes depend heavily on the capabilities of the controller. As manufacturing evolves toward intelligent, connected, and autonomous systems, traditional controllers often fall short, unable to keep pace with the demands of modern operations.

The EcoStruxure™ Automation Processor 310 (AP310) represents a shift in how industrial control is approached. It’s not just an upgrade, it’s a rethinking of what control systems can be. Designed to meet both current and future needs, the AP310 enables a new level of responsiveness, adaptability, and trustworthiness in automation environments.

Unlike legacy systems, the AP310 embraces open automation principles. It is built to integrate seamlessly, onboard securely, and operate reliably in dynamic, distributed architectures. The system is inherently secure, engineered to meet stringent cybersecurity standards, and designed to reduce complexity across the lifecycle, from deployment to maintenance.

Benefits

Trusted for today, engineered for tomorrow, some of the benefits include:

  • Helps ensure continuous operations and maximize plant uptime.

  • Helps avoid costly unplanned outages.

  • Helps protect capital investments (CAPEX) and reduce operating costs (OPEX) via an optimal lifecycle TOTEX.

  • Enhances overall system security, building trust in the system to help to reduce the risk and impact of cyber threats.

  • Helps reduce the overall number of controllers required.

Overview

The EcoStruxure™ Automation Processor 310 (AP310) is a rugged and secure edge automation platform designed to meet the evolving demands of industrial control environments. Purpose-built for deployment in harsh conditions, the AP310 serves as a Distributed Control Node (DCN) within the Foxboro SDA architecture, enabling next-generation automation capabilities at the edge.

New Features

Open Automation & Cybersecurity

  • Designed for modern intelligent manufacturing.

  • Built-in Trusted Platform Module for hardware-based security.

  • Cybersecurity Level 2 compliance

Designed for Industrial Deployment

  • Fanless cooling.

  • G3 conformal coating for harsh environments.

  • DIN rail mounting for easy installation.

  • Operating Temp: -20°C to +70°C.

EdgeOS – Customized Schneider RTOS

  • Schneider Electric-managed RTOS built on hardened Linux.

  • Designed for long-term support.

  • Supports Soft dPAC and Soft dPAC-HA configurations.

 Platform Diagnostics & Status

  • Monitoring of CPU temperature, load, memory, and network health.

  • Redfish-based diagnostics API for seamless integration with system management tools.

  • Enables proactive maintenance and operational visibility.

Trusted Platform Module (TPM)

  • Acts as a Root of Trust.

  • Stores cryptographic keys securely.

  • Verifies hardware/software integrity.

  • Helps prevent unauthorized access and verifies only trusted software is used.

ECC Memory

  • Equipped with LPDDR4 memory with in-band ECC support.

  • preserves data integrity and fault tolerance in critical operations.

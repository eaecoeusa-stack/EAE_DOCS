Reserve a Zone

Before reserving a zone, verify if the required zone is available.

Once done, perform the zone reservation steps depending on the type of actions you want to perform:

  • Edit the control application.

  • Deploy and diagnose a device.

Once the reservation of the zone is done, a message appears to inform you which zone is reserved. You can then see the reserved zone in the Reservation Overview dialog box.

NOTE: To know what is a zone and what is its purpose, refer to the Multi-User Zone Feature topic.

Verify if a Zone is Available

To verify that the zone is reserved by another user:

  • Check if the zone is listed in the tab All zone of the Reservation Overview dialog box.

  • Check visually in the Solution Explorer pad or in the editors if a reservation icon is appearing next to the zone name.

NOTE: Verify these ways also the sub-zones of the wanted zone as all sub-zones also need to be released.

Then if the zone or one of its sub-zones is reserved by another user, please contact this user through your own communication channel to request that user to release the needed zone.

NOTE: You can also directly reserve the zone, if that zone or one of its sub-zones is reserved by another user, a message informs you by whom the zone is currently reserved.

Reserve a Zone to Edit the Distributed Control Application

To reserve a zone to edit the Distributed Control Application you can:

  • Select the Reserve command on the element in the Solution Explorer pad as detailed here.

  • Open the wanted zone and click on the Reserve current zone in the Session menu.

  • Open the wanted zone and start editing, this automatically reserves the zone.

    NOTE: This reservation method only works with layers and resources.

Reserve a Zone to Deploy and Diagnose a Device

In the Deploy and Diagnostic editor, you can reserve a device to deploy and diagnose it by following these actions:

  • Manually reserve one or multiple devices as detailed here.

  • Click on the Deploy to Run Assistant button to perform actions on the device, this automatically reserves of the device.

    NOTE: This reservation method only works in local test.

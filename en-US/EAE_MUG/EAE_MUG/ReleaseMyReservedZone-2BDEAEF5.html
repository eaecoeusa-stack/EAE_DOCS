Release the Zone

Once the edits of a zone are delivered, or that no edit has been done on a zone, you can release the zone.

Releasing a zone:

  • Gives back to any user the rights to reserve this zone.

  • Removes you the rights to perform edit in the zone.

After releasing the zone, if any user, including you, wants to perform edit in the zone, that user needs to restart the Multi-User workflow.

To release a zone, follow one of these procedures depending on your case:

  • Release all your zones; Click on the End all reservations command in the Session menu.

  • Release the current zone; Click on the End reservation command in the Session menu.

  • Release one, couple or all of your zones:

    1. Open the My Zones tab of the Reservation Overview dialog box.

    2. Ensure that the zones you want to release are either in Delivered or NothingToDeliver status.

    3. Select the zones that you want to release.

    4. Click on the Release button.

  • Release one, couple or all zones of another user:

    1. Open the All Zones tab of the Reservation Overview dialog box.

    2. Select the zones that you want to release.

      NOTE: If the zones are in Unsaved or ReadyToDeliver status, then all the work done since the reservation will be deleted after the release. Thus, before releasing the zones, inform the concerned users to avoid unwanted data loss.

    3. Click on the Release button.

    NOTE: You can perform this procedure only if you are the owner of the Automation Solution in EcoStruxure Automation Expert Platform.

The wanted zones are then released and a message appears to inform you about the completion.

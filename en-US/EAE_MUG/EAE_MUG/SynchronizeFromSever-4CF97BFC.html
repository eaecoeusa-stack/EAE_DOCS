Update your Session

Once you have done the edits, verified and tested these edits, they are now compliant with your requirements.

Next, you should update your sessions. While you were doing these steps (editing, verifying and testing), some other users might also have done edits on the Distributed Control Application.

Once updated, test your edits again to check if they are integrated within the newest version of the Distributed Control Application.

To update your session, follow the next steps:

  1. Save the session.

    NOTE: If your session is not saved when clicking on the Update command, the Save Changes in System dialog box appears to ask if you want to save the current changes. It is recommended to save the changes before updating the session.

  2. Click on the Update command in the Session menu, if the Update command is enable.

    NOTE: To know more about the Update command, refer to Session menu.
    NOTE: After clicking on the Update command, if your Distributed Control Application get corrupted due to file conflict, the Working Copy corrupt pop up will appear. It is then recommended to follow these steps:

    1. Click on Copy to clipboard to open the corrupted folder in your OS system.

    2. Move to the parent folder containing this corrupted folder.

    3. Delete the corrupted folder.

    You can then restart EcoStruxure Automation Expert through EcoStruxure Automation Expert Platform. Your undelivered modifications might be lost.

After loading, the session is updated, you should test your edits with the newly added content.

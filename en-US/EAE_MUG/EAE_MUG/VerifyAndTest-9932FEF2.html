Verify and Test

After you have done and saved edits, or that you updated your session, it is recommended to:

  • Verify the edits, and

  • Test the edits

Verify the Edits

To verify the edits done, you can use the Verify command in the Home menu.

NOTE: To have more information about the Verify command, refer to the Home Menu topic of the EcoStruxure Automation Expert - User Manual.

Test the Edits

In this step you should test different aspect of the edits depending on which workflow’s step you come from:

  • Edit and Save Changes: Test that the edits are compliant with your requirements.

    Once the test is done, you can move to Update your Session step.

  • Update your Session: Test that the edits are integrated into the Distributed Control Application without interfering with what already exists.

    Once the test is done, you can move to Deliver Changes step.

To test the edits, you should test on your own the logic of the edits online.

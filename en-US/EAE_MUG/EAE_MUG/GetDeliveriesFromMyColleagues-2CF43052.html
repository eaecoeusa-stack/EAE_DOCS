Deliver Changes

Once the edits are done and tested in the latest update of the Distributed Control Application, you can then deliver these changes.

Delivering the edits allows to:

  • Save the edits on EcoStruxure Automation Expert Platform to prevent data loss.

  • Provide an appropriate commit message for each zone delivered.

  • Track the history of edits by the users on EcoStruxure Automation Expert Platform.

  • Share the edits with other users, as they are able to update their sessions and see the edits you did.

To deliver your edits, follow the next steps:

  1. Open the Reservation Overview dialog box.

  2. Ensure that the zone you want to deliver is in ReadyToDeliver state.

    If not, move back to the following step of the workflow: Verify and Test.

  3. Select the zone that you want to deliver (one, several or all zones).

  4. Click on the Deliver button.

    RESULT: It opens the Deliver my Modifications dialog box.

  5. Select all the zones to deliver.

  6. Write a commit message. This message will be visible in the Distributed Control Application history on EcoStruxure Automation Expert Platform.

  7. Click on the Deliver button.

    RESULT: The delivery of the edits starts.

Once the delivery is completed, a message appears to inform you about the completion.

Next, you can either:

  • Release the zone of the edits.

  • Perform other edits in this zone.

Working with Pads

The control and display elements for various work areas are located in the pads.

Pads can be docked to the margins in the main window. They can also be allowed to float on the screen.

When moving pads, potential docking points for drag and drop are displayed in the main window.

Moving a Pad

Step

Description

1

Click on the description of a pad with the left mouse button and hold it down.

2

Drag the mouse.

3

Possible docking points are displayed

4

Drag the pad to a docking point.

5

Release mouse button.

Taskbar

The task bar is located at the bottom of the EcoStruxure Automation Expert - Buildtime interface:

Task Bar Information

Area

Description

Status text area

Shows the status of the solution, including: Ready, Building, Build Failed, and so forth.

Progress area

Displays a progress bar for lengthy operations, for example Building IEC61499, Building HMI, and so forth.

Caret position

Shows the caret position in terms of line number, column number and character offset.

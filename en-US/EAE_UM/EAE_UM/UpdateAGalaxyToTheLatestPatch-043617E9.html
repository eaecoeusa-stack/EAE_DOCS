2.5 Update a Galaxy to the Latest Patch

Here are the steps to continue the Asset Link for AVEVA OMI workflow from EcoStruxure Automation Expert in:

  • A new solution

  • An existing solution with a Galaxy already configured

A New Solution

In the Solution Explorer pad of the new solution, right click on your project. Next, select Configurations > AVEVA SP Configuration to open the System Platform Configuration dialog box.

Then follow the steps depending your case:

  • Create a new Galaxy

    1. Click on New Galaxy with System Objects.

    2. Write the Galaxy name.

    3. Click on Next.

    4. Update the required naming for the System Objects, App Objects and Communication Objects.

    5. Click on Create.

    RESULT: The Galaxy is created.

  • Choose an existing Galaxy

    1. Click on Select Galaxy with System Objects.

    2. Click on the drop down list.

    3. Select a Galaxy from the list.

    4. Enter the Username and Password.

    5. Click on Validate.

    6. When the validation is complete, click on Next.

    7. Configure the System Objects, App Objects and Communication Objects.

    8. Click on Save.

    Once saved, proceed with the Asset Link workflow.

An Existing Solution with a Galaxy Already Configured

Follow the next steps in the Deploy and Diagnostic editor:

  1. Click on the drop down list of actions of the AVEVA server.

  2. Select one of the two following actions:

    • Sync to AVEVA System Platform > Sync Changes

    • Sync to AVEVA System Platform > Sync Selection

    RESULT: The Galaxy Credential dialog box opens.

  3. In the Choose existing section, click on Validate.

Once the validation is complete, the AVEVA server device sync operation proceeds.

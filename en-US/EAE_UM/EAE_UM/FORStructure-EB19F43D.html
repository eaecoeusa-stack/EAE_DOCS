3. FOR Structure

Structured text defines a FOR-loop as a control structure, with which you can execute a group of statements (ST statements) with a certain number of repetitions.

Syntax of the FOR control structure: 


FOR <VARIABLE := <ANY_INT TO <ANY_INT [BY <ANY_INT>] DO
  <ST Statements>
END_FOR;
Example 1: 
FOR index := 0 TO 10 DO
  out0 := out0 + 1;
END_FOR;
Example 2: 
FOR index := 0 TO 10 BY 2 DO
  out0 := out0 + 1;
END_FOR;

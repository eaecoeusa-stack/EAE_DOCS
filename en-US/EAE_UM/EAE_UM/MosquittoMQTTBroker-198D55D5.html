MQTT Broker

For the MQTT function block or MQTT diagnostics communication, an MQTT broker with specific requirements is required:

  • To communicate with the MQTT client, and

  • To perform device diagnostics.

Two options are available to get an MQTT broker:

  • Get and configure your own MQTT broker that meets the requirements.

  • Complete the steps to configure an MQTT broker Mosquitto:

    1. Install Mosquitto.

    2. Secure Mosquitto Communication.

    3. Configure Mosquitto.

    4. (If required) Define multiple Mosquitto Listeners.

    NOTE: The procedure is given for Mosquitto, but there is no obligation to use this specific MQTT broker. To know more about Mosquitto, check its official website here.

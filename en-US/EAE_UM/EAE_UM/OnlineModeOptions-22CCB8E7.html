Online Mode Options

Overview

If your Solution is in online mode and your diagram is active, more options are available in the State Graph and Sequence Diagram pads.

Mapping and resource initialization

 CAUTION
the sequences may not be initialized correctly.
When mapping a Procedural CAT to a resource, make sure the SEQ_INIT of the EVENTCHAINHEAD block has been initialized in the targeted resource. For more information refer to the SE.AppSequence library documentation.
Failure to follow these instructions can result in injury or equipment damage.

Designing the Physical Architecture

The Physical Devices Editor gives access to a catalog of devices allowing you to design the physical network of your application in the workspace. To design it, follow the next aspects of Designing the Physical Architecture:

  • Link devices together by connecting device physical Ethernet ports.

  • Manage and configure logical networks and assign IP addresses to logical network devices.

  • Show or hide device configuration settings, including name and IP address.

  • Associate physical and logical devices by assigning device types.

Library Dependencies

This topic describes the dependencies between libraries delivered with EcoStruxure Automation Expert. The following libraries do not depend on other libraries; they are used by all the other libraries:

  • IEC61131.Standard

  • Runtime.Base

  • SE.Standard.

Special types of library: vertical applications (Conveying, LiquidFood, WWW), communications (those dealing with portal, Modbus, EthernetIP, OPC UA).

U: the library in the row is referenced by the one in the column. ∩: the library in the row references the one in the column. O: the two libraries are often jointly used.

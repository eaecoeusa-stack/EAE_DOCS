EcoStruxure Automation Expert Platform

EcoStruxure Automation Expert Platform v25.0 requires EcoStruxure Automation Expert v25.0 to open a Distributed Control Application.

EcoStruxure Automation Expert v25.0 is compatible with EcoStruxure Automation Expert Platform v25.0.

NOTE: In case of difficulty related to the compatibility of EcoStruxure Automation Expert Platform and EcoStruxure Automation Expert, please contact the Schneider Electric support team.

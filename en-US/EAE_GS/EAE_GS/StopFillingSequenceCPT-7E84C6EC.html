Stopping the Filling Sequence

So far, with what have been developed in the section Start Filling Sequence, the sequence will:

  • Set in position all the instruments.

  • Fill the tank until the expected level of whole milk is reached.

  • Close the valves and turn OFF the agitator when the expected level is reached.

The happy path of the sequence is then correctly developed.

However, if something unexpected happens or if the operator wants to stop the sequence before the level is reached, then the sequence cannot handle these situations.

To be able to stop anytime the Filling sequence, then a second sequence has to be developed, the Stop Filling Sequence.

The Stop Filling Sequence is then defined as followed:

Step Action Transition Condition
Start The Filling Sequence has received the command to stop.
Step 1 The TR1V01 and TR1V02 valves are command to close. Valves TR1V01 and TR1V02 are closed.
Step 2 The agitator is commanded to turn OFF. Agitator is turned OFF.
NOTE:

The two steps of the Stop Filling sequence are the same as the fifth and sixth steps of the Filling Sequence, seen in the topic Filling Sequence Definition, since in both cases they serve the same purpose, stopping the filling process.

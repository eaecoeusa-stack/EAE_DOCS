IoEmbeddedATV6xx DigitalInputs

Abstract

IoEmbeddedATV6xx DigitalInputs faceplate allows you to monitor the Digital Inputs channels of the IoEmbeddedATV6xx.

You can monitor and force the Digital Inputs by means of IoEmbeddedATV6xx DigitalInputs faceplate.

 WARNING
LOSS OF CONTROL
  • Verify that forcing the value does not result in unsafe conditions.
  • Verify that the fact to close the tab while a forced value is active does not result in unsafe conditions.
Failure to follow these instructions can result in death, serious injury, or equipment damage.

Description

Usage

This faceplate opens when the corresponding active zone is clicked in the IoEmbeddedATV6xx HMI.

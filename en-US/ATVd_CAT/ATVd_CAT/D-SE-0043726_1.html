[Modbus HMI] (Md2-) Menu

Access

[Communication] [Comm parameters] [Modbus SL] [Modbus HMI]

About This Menu

This menu is related to the Modbus serial communication port on the front of the control block. It is used by default for the Display Terminal. The Display Terminal is only compliant with the following settings: [HMI Baud Rate] (tbr2) equal to [19200 bps] (19k2), [Term 2 word order] (tWO2) equal to [HIGH] (On) and [HMI Format] (tFO2) equal to [8-E-1] (8E1).

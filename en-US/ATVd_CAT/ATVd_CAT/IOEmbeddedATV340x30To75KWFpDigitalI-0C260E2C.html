IoEmbeddedATV340Ex30to75kW DigitalInputs

Abstract

IoEmbeddedATV340Ex30to75kW DigitalInputs faceplate allows you to monitor the Digital Output channels of the IoEmbeddedATV340.

You can monitor and force the Digital Outputs by means of IoEmbeddedATV340Ex30to75kW DigitalInputs faceplate.

 WARNING
LOSS OF CONTROL
  • Verify that forcing the value does not result in unsafe conditions.
  • Verify that the fact to close the tab while a forced value is active does not result in unsafe conditions.
Failure to follow these instructions can result in death, serious injury, or equipment damage.

Description

Usage

This faceplate opens when the corresponding active zone is clicked in the IoEmbeddedATV340Ex30to75kW HMI.

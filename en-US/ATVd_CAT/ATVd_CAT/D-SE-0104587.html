ATVSpeedControl - fpDriveTrend

Abstract

ATVSpeedControl - fpDriveTrend provides a graphical representation of the various Drive parameter values.

You can monitor and observe the values of various drive parameters such as Motor speed, Motor frequency, Motor current and Motor torque.

Description

Usage

You can access the ATVSpeedControl - fpDriveTrend faceplate, when you click on the corresponding active zone in the ATVSpeedControl - sDrive.

Faceplate Components

The figure below illustrates the ATVSpeedControl - fpDriveTrend faceplate:

Click and select the following parameters to observe and monitor their values in the graphical representation:

  • Motor Speed RPM

  • Motor Speed-Ref RPM

  • Motor Frequency Hz

  • Motor Current A

  • Motor Torque Pct

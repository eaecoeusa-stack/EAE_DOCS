Overview

EcoStruxure Automation Expert - Archive can be installed on a Harmony iPC or a PC, running Microsoft Windows. It can also be installed on a Harmony iPC HMIP63G10N30BE3N00 or a PC, running Linux Ubuntu or Debian operating system.

The EcoStruxure Automation Expert - Archive (Windows) can be installed during the installation of EcoStruxure Automation Expert - Runtime, by selecting it from the list of features.

The EcoStruxure Automation Expert - Archive (Ubuntu and Debian) installer is available in the Archive subfolder of the EcoStruxure Automation Expert installation package.

The following image shows how the logical devices are connected between them, and how they are linked to the physical devices in your EcoStruxure Automation Expert - Buildtime solution:

The variable and alarm data, collected from the connected devices, configured in your solution, is stored in a PostgreSQL database. An Archive link, which is created and configured in EcoStruxure Automation Expert, enables the connection between the devices used on the solution and the Archive database.

Viewing and Managing the Archived Data

Overview

You can use archived variable values and alarms, collected from the configured devices in your solution, in the following ways:

  • To monitor single historized variable value, in the EcoStruxure Automation Expert - HMI.

  • To monitor groups of variable values in the EcoStruxure Automation Expert - HMI, using a history trend configuration.

  • To view historized alarms in the EcoStruxure Automation Expert - HMI.

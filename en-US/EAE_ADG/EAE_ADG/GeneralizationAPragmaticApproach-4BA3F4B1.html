Generalization: A Pragmatic Approach

A typical process is usually made up of several groups of devices within which the devices are highly inter-connected, but that are loosely coupled between them (connected graphs). This structure can be represented by a logical network using the AND operator to combine dependent devices or groups and the OR operator to allow for independence.

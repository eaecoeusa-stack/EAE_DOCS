EM_C1 Equipment module

As detailed in the design guideline, the EM_C1 equipment modules operate as a child sub-system module. Thus, its internal composition is identical to its parent module, the Unit 601 CAT object.

This chapter then focus on modeling the EM_C1 objects by providing a comprehensive identification of all core components involved.

The EM_C1 equipment module is the most fundamental and final sub-system in the hierarchical structure of object aggregation of this use case.

Preparation Procedure

Perform the preparation procedure for Unit 600 before the plant startup.

  1. Utilities check:

    • Instrument air:

      • The compressor is working.

      • Instrument air is present at Unit 600 (check the line pressure).

    • Chilled water:

      • Chiller is working.

      • Chilled water distribution pump is working.

      • Check that the cooling water supply valves to CO1-C1, CO2-C1, and E-PV1 are open.

    • Nitrogen:

      • The nitrogen tank is not empty.

      • The nitrogen tank is connected to the nitrogen distribution line.

      • Check nitrogen pressure on the nitrogen distribution line.

      • Check the pressure reducer set at Unit 600.

    • Tap water:

      • Tap water line is connected to D-2.

  2. Turn on electric cabinets.

  3. Check and reset emergency pushbuttons, if required.

  4. Check tanks level:

    • D-1, mother liquor tank:

      • Has to be full.

    • D-2:

      • Set 6XV-001 in automatic mode.

      • Monitor D-2 filling (and overflow).

    • D-5, distilled MEG tank:

      • Has to be empty.

    • D-6, distillation column bottom tank:

      • Has to be empty.

    • D-7, thin film evaporator waste tank:

      • Has to be empty.

    • D-8, distilled water tank:

      • Has to be empty.

  5. Turn on electric cabinets.

  6. Check and reset emergency pushbuttons, if required.

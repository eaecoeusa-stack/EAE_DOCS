End Point Protection

End Point Protection is managed by one of CAP components: Acronis® Cyber Protect.

NOTE: For more information about CAP, refer to the topic Cybersecurity Application Platform.
NOTE: Use of Acronis® Cyber Protect requires a license that includes the end point protection feature. For more information, refer to its website.

Protection plans should be defined for all the workstations, servers and VMs in the system. To help protect the backups against tampering, they must have encryption and "Backup verification" enabled.

The Protection plans should include virus protection by "Windows Defender Antivirus". For additional security, you can use the McAfee collection of security products.

The Acronis® Cyber Protect component:

  • Allows for continuous protection of engineering workstations inside network levels 2 and 3 Control Room & Operations.

  • Needs a licence that includes the endpoint protection feature.

Acronis® Cyber Protect consists of:

  • A server component hosted inside the DMZ network, and accessible within CAP.

  • Agents installed on every engineering workstation that benefits from end point protection. Agents can be installed on Windows or Ubuntu operating systems.

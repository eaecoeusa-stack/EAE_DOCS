Defense in Depth

No single cybersecurity approach is adequate on its own.

That is why Schneider Electric recommends a defense in depth strategy.

NOTE: To learn about Schneider Electric defense in depth strategy, check the Defense In Depth Strategy topic.

Defense in depth, also known as multi-layer security, is a security strategy that provides protection mechanisms at various levels in order prevent or slow down an adversary from exploiting the system. The protection layers range from securing the physical room and equipment in a facility to the actions performed by an operator on a computer in a control room.

EcoStruxure Automation Expert offer is a multi-tier, multi-level system comprising divergent components and sub-systems made up of communication networks, control devices, servers, and software applications that reside at different layers for the smooth operation of the plant. EcoStruxure Automation Expert implements security at various layers in order to help provide essential security protection to the entire Control System.

The defense in depth approach, conceived by the United States National Security Agency (NSA), layers the network with security features, appliances, and processes.

NOTE: For more information, read the U.S. Department of Homeland Security document titled Recommended Practice: Improving Industrial Control System Cybersecurity with Defense in Depth Strategies.
